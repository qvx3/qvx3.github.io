Good learning is supported by spelling words phone after phone, that is, pronouncing syllables containing a phone corresponding to each letter. In order to standardize this, such syllable-spelling takes place according to such sign → sound conversion:

  • vowel → vowel
  • hh → hehe
  • single consonant → this consonant + e
  • another digraph → this digraph + e
  • isolate q, v, x → ku, falv, iks
Here, all letter and digraph names are finally needed. These came to life using abovementioned conversion.
Few in red are supplemental: needed only for enhanced precision i.e. in foreign speech transliteration.
